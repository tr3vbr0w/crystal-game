Crystal Collector!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. Clicking a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the gam if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will challenge the values of each crystal.
